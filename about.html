About

K Barnes Construction has been involved in the construction industry for the past 30 years and with this time and experience we have the capability and track record to perform any task. Located in Courtown, Co. Wexford. The majority of work that we carry out range from Wexford to Dublin. Whether the job is a new build, an extension or a renovation K Barnes Construction Services can meet any challenge. We take great interest in listening to our clients and to ensure that we fully understand the project that will be undertaking and any concerns that the client has about particular aspects of the project in hand.
